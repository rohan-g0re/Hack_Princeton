## üéØ Design Principles Applied

1. **Consistency** - Same header, footer, and styling across all pages
2. **Clarity** - Clear visual hierarchy and information structure
3. **Branding** - Strong MealPilot identity throughout
4. **Professionalism** - Clean, modern design with attention to detail
5. **User Experience** - Intuitive navigation and clear call-to-actions
6. **Visual Feedback** - Hover effects, badges, and status indicators
7. **Color Psychology** - Browns for trust/reliability, green for success
